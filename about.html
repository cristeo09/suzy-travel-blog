Hi, I'm Suzy!

I'm a passionate travel blogger currently based in the beautiful, sun-drenched city of Lisbon. What started as a small diary to keep my friends and family updated on my post-college backpacking trip has evolved into 'Wander With Suzy'—a full-time pursuit of documenting the world's most incredible cultures, flavors, and hidden gems.

When I'm not navigating chaotic street markets in Bangkok or hiking the verdant rice terraces of Bali, you can find me wandering the steep cobblestone streets of Alfama, hunting down the perfect pastel de nata. My mission is to share authentic stories and practical guides to inspire you to pack your bags and experience the unknown.
